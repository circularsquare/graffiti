graffiti nyc

Paint every surface in New York City!

Your paint will be visible for all to see. Unless someone paints over it. Please don't paint anything inappropriate!! >_<

Data sources:

  • Buildings: NYC DoITT 3D Building Model (2014) (CityGML)
  • Terrain: USGS 3DEP 1 m DEM
  • Street map: OpenStreetMap via Overpass
  • Trees: NYC LiDAR tree dataset (Scientific Data, 2023)

Rendered with Three.js and WebGL. Each building face is split into 1m paintable cells. The terrain is 2m cells.

The building data is from 2014, so buildings younger than that are not present. Also I apologize if the site runs slowly, a fast internet connection and some gpu are necessary.
