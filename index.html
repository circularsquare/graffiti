graffiti nyc

Paint every surface in New York City!

Your paint will be visible forever. Unless someone paints over it. Please don't paint anything inappropriate or i will ban you!! >_<

Data sources:

  • Buildings: NYC DoITT 3D Building Model (2014) (CityGML)
  • Terrain: USGS 3DEP 1 m DEM
  • Street map: OpenStreetMap via Overpass

Rendered with Three.js and WebGL. Each building face is split into 1m paintable cells. The terrain is 2m cells. Other people's paints will not show live, but will generally update within ~10 seconds. I apologize if the site runs slowly, a fast internet connection and some gpu are necessary.
